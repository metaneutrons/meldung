# Reporting Data Protection and IT Security Incidents

Welcome! Here you can quickly and securely report security incidents or data protection breaches to the responsible staff.

## When is a report required?
Please report the following events in particular:
* **Suspected unauthorized access** to systems, accounts, or data.
* **Loss or theft** of devices (laptops, smartphones, USB drives) containing confidential data.
* **Security-relevant incidents** such as phishing emails, social engineering, or malware infections (viruses, ransomware).
* **Anomalies**, such as unusual system behavior or sudden IT outages.
* **Data breaches**, e.g., accidental transmission of confidential information to the wrong recipients.

## Important Notes
* **Speed matters:** The sooner you inform us, the more effectively we can limit the damage. For incidents involving **personal data**, we are legally required to report them to the supervisory authorities within **72 hours** (Art. 33 GDPR). Please support us by reporting as early as possible, as time is needed after your report for assessment and preparation of information before submission to the authorities.
* **Auto-save:** You can interrupt the form at any time; your progress is automatically saved in your browser.
* **Don't forget to submit:** We only receive the information once you submit. You can skip intermediate steps. Please complete the process promptly and report additional information afterwards if needed.

## How to proceed
1. **Fill in the form:** Provide as many details as possible. Required fields are marked, but even incomplete information helps us.
2. **Submit:** Be sure to complete the process by clicking "Submit", even if you cannot answer all questions.
3. **Documentation:** After submission, you will receive a ticket number and can download your report as a PDF for your records.
4. **Availability:** Please remain reachable via the contact details you provided, in case follow-up questions are needed for the analysis.
