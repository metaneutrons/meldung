# Report an IT Security Incident

Use this form to report an IT security incident or a data protection breach.

## When should you report?

- Suspected unauthorized access to systems or data
- Loss or theft of devices containing sensitive data
- Phishing attacks or social engineering
- Malware infections (viruses, ransomware, trojans)
- Unusual system behavior or outages
- Accidental disclosure of confidential information

## Important Notes

- **Report promptly** — the sooner you report, the faster countermeasures can be initiated.
- **If personal data is involved**, there is a legal reporting deadline of **72 hours** (Art. 33 GDPR).
- Your report will be treated confidentially.
- You can interrupt the form at any time — your progress is saved automatically.

## Process

1. Complete the form steps (approx. 5–10 minutes)
2. After submission you will receive a reference number
3. The IT security team will contact you
